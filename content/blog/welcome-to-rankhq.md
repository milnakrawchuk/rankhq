Automation is useful right up until it starts publishing confident nonsense that a real local business has to live with.

That is why RankHQ uses a constrained content workflow instead of a loose "AI writer" setup. Every article starts from a topic queue for local service businesses, moves through a fixed template, and must pass validation before it can be published.

## The rules are simple

- No invented facts
- No fake case studies
- No unsourced medical, legal, or financial claims
- No publish if required inputs are missing

## Why this matters

Daily publishing only compounds if quality stays intact. Thin, generic city pages and fake expertise pieces create cleanup debt. Strong systems reduce that risk by making the safe path the default path.

## What ships with each article

Every publishable local-service article needs:

- a clear topic and intent
- a summary description
- a canonical slug
- title and metadata
- a real CTA
- source links when claims require support

## The real goal

The goal is not "post every day no matter what."

The goal is to post every day when the article is strong enough to deserve being live and useful for a real local business.
