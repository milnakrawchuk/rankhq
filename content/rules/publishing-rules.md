# Publishing Rules

- No invented facts.
- No fake case studies, testimonials, or client outcomes.
- No medical, legal, or financial claims unless the supporting sources are listed in frontmatter.
- No fake local market data, rankings, lead counts, or conversion lifts.
- No city pages or service pages that are just spun copies with place-name swaps.
- Do not publish if title, description, published date, CTA, or body are missing.
- Do not publish if the article is still marked `draft: true`.
- Do not publish if the topic queue entry is incomplete.
- Prefer one strong article over one weak daily post.
